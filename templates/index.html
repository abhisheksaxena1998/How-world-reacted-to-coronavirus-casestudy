What was world's reaction on Twitter during Covid-19 pandemic


Time duration for study
From 1 November 2019 to 30 May 2020


  • This case study investigates "What was world’s reaction on Twitter in period of Coronavirus". At this critical time, when whole world is witnessing Covid-19 pandemic. A total tweet over the period of 7 months from Twitter social media related to the coronavirus hashtags. And these tweets were classified as hate speech or offensive language and then analyzed for correlation among various factors. The motto is to find out the what is overall sentiment of whole world in this tough time.
